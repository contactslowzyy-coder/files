FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our console tweaks.

What do these tweaks actually do?
These tweaks are built to reduce input delay, lower ping, and improve FPS stability. By optimizing system and network settings, they make your inputs feel more responsive, your connection more stable, and your gameplay noticeably smoother. Everything is tweaked to enhance performance without compromising system safety.
Do I need any tools or downloads for console tweaks?
Nope — everything is done using your console's built-in settings and a few optional router tweaks (like DNS or port forwarding for better ping). No downloads required.
Is my device supported?
Our tweaks are compatible with every Xbox, PS5 and PS4, ensuring broad support across all major consoles.
Can this improve my Wi-Fi connection?
It helps — but for best results, use a wired (Ethernet) connection. The tweaks will still improve consistency and reduce jitter over Wi-Fi, though.
Are tweaks bad for long-term use?
We take pride in providing stability and reliability for our customers. We don't do anything extreme that could damage your console or shorten its lifespan. All tweaks are safe for long-term use.
Is it safe to use?
Absolutely. All tweaks have been carefully developed with a strong focus on both performance and safety. Each release is thoroughly tested to ensure it does not interfere with core system files or introduce any risks. You can use them with full confidence — they are safe, effective, and reliable.
